Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our most common questions.

How do I start my new game server?

Once your order is complete, your server will be provisioned instantly. You will receive an email with your control panel login details. From there, you can start, stop, and configure your server with just a few clicks. Check our Knowledge Base for game-specific setup guides.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 24-hour refund policy for all new game server and VPS hosting plans. If you are not satisfied with our service for any reason, please submit a support ticket within 24 hours of your initial purchase to request a full refund. Please see our Refund Policy page for full details.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time through our client area. The process is automated, and you will only be charged a prorated amount for the difference. Your data and server IP address will remain unchanged.

Do you offer DDoS protection?

Absolutely. All our services come with enterprise-grade DDoS protection to keep your server online and safe from malicious attacks. Our system automatically detects and mitigates attacks without impacting your server's performance.

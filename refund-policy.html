Refund Policy

Our commitment to customer satisfaction includes a clear and fair refund policy.

Last Updated: July 28, 2025

1. Overview

At CoRamTix, we strive to ensure our customers are satisfied with our services. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which CoRamTix may provide a refund, the process for claiming a refund, and the responsibility of CoRamTix in cases of a refund request.

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer a 48-hour money-back guarantee on all new hosting service orders. If you are not satisfied with our service for any reason during the first 48 hours of your service activation, you are eligible for a full refund.

Conditions for Refund:

  • The refund request must be submitted within 48 hours of the service activation time.
  • The policy applies only to new customers and new hosting service orders. Renewals are not eligible for a refund.
  • The account must be in good standing and not in violation of our Terms of Service.

3. Non-Refundable Services

Certain products and services are not eligible for refunds under this policy. These include:

  • Domain Name Registrations/Transfers: Due to the policies of domain registrars, we cannot offer refunds for domain name registrations or transfers. Once a domain is registered, it is registered for the full term.
  • Software Licenses: Any software licenses purchased through CoRamTix (e.g., cPanel, WHM, etc.) are non-refundable.
  • Dedicated Servers: Dedicated servers involve significant upfront costs and are therefore not eligible for the 48-hour money-back guarantee.
  • Service Add-ons: Any additional services or add-ons, such as dedicated IP addresses or management services, are non-refundable.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you must submit a cancellation request through our client area and open a support ticket with our billing department specifying your refund request. All requests must be submitted within the 48-hour eligibility window.

Please include the following information in your support ticket:

  • Your full name and contact information.
  • The primary domain name on the account.
  • The reason for your cancellation and refund request.

5. Processing Refunds

Once we receive your refund request, we will review it to ensure it meets the eligibility criteria. If approved, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Please note that your financial institution may take additional time to reflect the transaction in your account.

6. Policy Changes

CoRamTix reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact our billing department through a support ticket in the client area or email us at:

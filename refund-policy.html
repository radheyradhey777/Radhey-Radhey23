Refund Policy

Last Updated: August 9, 2025

At CoRamTix, we strive to ensure our customers are satisfied with our services. This policy outlines the conditions under which we offer refunds for our hosting products.

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer a 24-hour (1-day) money-back guarantee on all new hosting service purchases. If you are not satisfied with your service for any reason, you may request a full refund within the first 72 hours of the initial service activation.

Services Eligible for Refund:

  • New Game Hosting Server plans.
  • New Bot Hosting Server plans.
  • New VPS Hosting Server plans.

Services NOT Eligible for Refund:

  • Service Renewals: Refunds are not available for any recurring or renewal payments. It is the customer's responsibility to cancel their service before the renewal date if they do not wish to continue.
  • Addons and Licenses: Purchases of dedicated IP addresses, software licenses (e.g., cPanel, WHMCS), and other one-time addons are non-refundable.
  • Custom Work or Setups: Any fees for custom configurations, server management, or administrative work are non-refundable.
  • Violation of Terms of Service: Accounts terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Service are not eligible for a refund.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you must submit a support ticket through our official client area within the 72-hour eligibility window. The ticket must include your service details and the reason for your refund request.

  • Refund requests made via email, Discord, or any other method will not be considered.
  • The 72-hour period begins at the exact time of service activation as recorded in our billing system.

3. Refund Processing

Once your refund request is received and approved, we will process the refund to your original payment method within 5-10 business days. Please note that it may take additional time for the funds to appear in your account, depending on your financial institution.

Any service for which a refund is issued will be terminated immediately upon processing the refund.

4. Chargebacks and Disputes

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute with our payment processor or your financial institution is a violation of our Terms of Service. Doing so will result in the immediate suspension of all your active services with us and may lead to the permanent termination of your account. We encourage you to contact our support team first to resolve any billing issues.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact our support team before making a purchase.
